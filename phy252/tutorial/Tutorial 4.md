# Question 7
---
a.
Constant volume, no work done

$\Delta Q=\Delta \left< E \right>$ in this case.
$$
	P_{i}V_{i} - P_{f}V_{f} = 1-5=-4
$$
Convert from atmospheres to pascals and litres to cubic metres.
$$
	\Delta Q = 4 \times \frac{101325}{1} \times \frac{1}{1000} \approx 405.3 \text{ Joules}
$$

---
b.
Solve for the final pressure,
$$
	P_{i}V_{i}^{\gamma} = P_{f}V_{f}^{\gamma} \implies P_{f} = \frac{P_{i}V_{i}^{\gamma}}{V_{f}^{\gamma}} = \frac{1}{10^{\gamma}} \approx 2.14 \times 10^{-2} \text{ atm}
$$
The work is:
$$
	W = \frac{P_{f}V_{f}-P_{i}V_{i}}{\gamma-1} \approx -1.17 = -118.90 \text{ Joules}
$$
Adiabatic process, so there is no heat transfer.
