I didn't know how to transform $\begin{pmatrix}n-2 \\  k-2\end{pmatrix}$ into $\begin{pmatrix}n \\  k\end{pmatrix}$ so I basically just defined this letter $A$ such that:
$$
	\begin{pmatrix}
	n \\
	k
	\end{pmatrix} = A \begin{pmatrix}
	n-2 \\
	k-2
	\end{pmatrix}
$$
And then completed the problem that way to get another binomial distribution.
